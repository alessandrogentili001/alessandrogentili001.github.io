About Me

Hello! I'm Alessandro, a passionate student of Artificial Intelligence at the University of Bologna. My academic journey has equipped me with a strong foundation in machine learning, data analysis, and software development.

I'm particularly interested in developing AI solutions that can solve real-world problems, especially in industrial scenarios, and make a positive impact on society. When I'm not coding or studying, you can find me working out, reading, cooking, or trying to convince my friends to start a new project.

My approach to AI combines technical expertise with a deep curiosity about how these technologies can enhance human capabilities rather than replace them. I believe in building systems that are not only intelligent but also ethical, transparent, and accessible.

Current Work

  • Researching efficient implementations of transformer models for resource-constrained environments
  • Exploring applications of computer vision in manufacturing quality control
  • Mentoring junior students in machine learning fundamentals

Future Goals

  • Contribute to open-source AI projects that focus on sustainability and social good
  • Publish research on making deep learning more accessible to small businesses
  • Develop expertise in AI ethics and responsible innovation
  • Build a community of AI practitioners focused on practical applications
